Burrito

We offer a delectable vegan, vegetarian, non-gmo option with our beauty in simplicity, two ingredient burrito.

This no frills option needs nothing more when executed to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of refried beans
  • 1 tortilla

Procedure

  1. Prep beans by opening can.
  2. Place tortilla on plate and warm in microwave for 15 seconds.
  3. Remove beans from can and lay in center of tortilla.
  4. Wrap bean log with care.
  5. Enjoy.
