Crispy chicken with potato puck

Home-range chicken, breaded and air fried to offer a best of both worlds health experience.

Pair this with potatoes grown in our rooftop garden, skinned, mashed and molded into a bite size bit, also served crispy, your taste buds will soar.

Ingredients

  • 1-2 home-range chicken breast
  • 2-3 potatoes

Procedure

  1. Dice chicken into nuggets.
  2. Trim then boil potatoes until fork tender.
  3. Bread and season chicken in flour and egg combo station.
  4. Remove potatoes from boil and mash. Form into bite size pucks.
  5. In oven, bake for 8 hours until well done.
  6. Enjoy.
