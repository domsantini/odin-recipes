💩🍔😉 COOKBOOK

Charcuterie board with imported meats and cheese

For an upscale small bite, explore Europe's finest offerings of cured meat and aged cheese.

No need to be good all the time, we add a biscuit baked in-house and artisan crisp to layer your flavor journey onto.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of your favorite imported cured meat
  • 1 lb of your favorite imported aged cheese
  • 1 box of artisan crisps
  • 1-10 cookies purchased from our bakery

Procedure

  1. Trim meat into fine slivers. Lay meat ribbons on top of each other in large pile.
  2. Cut section of cheese and serve with knife, or mirror trimmed meat thickness.
  3. Remove handful of crisps from box. Lay besides pile of meat and cheese.
  4. Sneak a cookie for yourself. Place remaining cookies onto plate.
  5. Enjoy.
